Cube Cream:
The future
of Ice Cream
Served 24/7
in a Cube!

Born in Kansas City, Missouri, Cube Cream is re-imagining the way we experience ice cream.

Imagine walking up to a sleek, cube-shaped kiosk at any time of the day or night. With just a tap on the touchscreen, you choose from six delicious flavors of ice cream and eight fun toppings. Then, right before your eyes, a robotic arm crafts your dessert — cutting a perfect cube of ice cream, adding your favorite sprinkles, chips, or candies, and serving it up in a stylish cup.

Extruder Technology

Our custom made extrudes allow us to serve quality thick ice cream, in a cube, better than soft serve.

24/7 Availability

Always open, always ready to serve.

Six Flavor Options

From classics like vanilla and chocolate to rotating specialties.
